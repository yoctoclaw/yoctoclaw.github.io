About KrillClaw

KrillClaw is the smallest AI agent runtime for edge devices — a 49KB binary written in Zig with zero dependencies, no OS requirement, and compatibility with any OpenAI-compatible LLM provider. It brings autonomous AI agents to hardware as small as a $3 ESP32, enabling real-time sensor monitoring, device control, and intelligent automation on devices that were previously too constrained for AI. Tested on 350+ hardware configurations from smart rings to industrial sensors, KrillClaw ships in two profiles: Lite (49KB, BLE/serial) for ultra-constrained devices and Full (≤500KB, HTTP/TLS) for autonomous operation.

About Accelerando AI

Accelerando AI is a research and product studio building infrastructure for embodied intelligence. Founded on the principle that AI should run everywhere — not just in the cloud — Accelerando develops tools that bring autonomous agents to the physical world. KrillClaw is the company's first shipping product. Accelerando AI holds pending patents on edge AI agent architecture.
