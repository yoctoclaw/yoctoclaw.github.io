Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

KrillClaw™ is an open-source, on-device AI agent runtime built by Accelerando AI. We take privacy seriously — it's core to why this product exists.

What KrillClaw collects

Nothing. KrillClaw runs entirely on your hardware. No telemetry, no analytics, no phone-home. Your device, your data.

What this website collects

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily sign up for the waitlist. Used solely to notify you when pre-flashed devices are available.
  • GitHub Pages hosting — GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type). See GitHub's privacy statement.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services on this website.

LLM provider data

KrillClaw connects to LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) that you configure. Data sent to those providers is governed by their respective privacy policies. KrillClaw does not route your data through any intermediary — it connects directly from your device to the provider you choose.

Your rights

Want your email removed from the waitlist? Email us and we'll delete it immediately. No questions asked.
